Homing Instinct
回帰願望

2012
Mineral pigment and dyed mud pigment on polyester cloth
ポリエステル画布，岩絵具，水干絵具
diptych, 920 * 1830mm each / ディプティック，各920 * 1830mm





Related : HOTサンダルプロジェクト2012

Marugame city of Kagawa prefecture offered a studio at islands in Seto Inland Sea to students for 1 month.
During the project, We had some workshops with residents of surrounding islands, and works were exhibited in a city gallery after that.

香川県丸亀市の支援により瀬戸内海・小手島に１ヶ月間滞在し，取材・制作した．
期間中には周辺の島民の方との交流やワークショップも行われ，その後，作品は同市内にて展示された．
